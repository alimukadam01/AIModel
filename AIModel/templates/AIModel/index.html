A bit about Karachi.

Karachi has been an important city for over 100 years. Being a central hub for most economic and political activity of the country, it has been a host to many. Karachi is the most populous city of Pakistan, a home to many different communities and cultures. Also known as the City of Lights, the developing mega-city has attracted people from all over the world in search of jobs, better education, business meetings and many other facilities that the city has to offer. Low cost treatments from the multiple Public Hospitals in Karachi are received by patients across the country. It also offers an exposure to a mixed variety of cultures and traditions, people from all gentries and languages leaving you memories that are one to carry.
The gentle sea breeze makes evenings in Karachi extra pleasant, made more exciting by the hustle and bustle around the city which continues well after sundown. ATMs, Pharmacies, food deliveries and most hotels are open 24 hours. Markets are open till midnight while several food streets, restaurants, cafes, and eateries serve customers even in the wee hours. The city is in a state of constant stir at all times of the day which brings out your high spirits, making stays all the more memorable!
